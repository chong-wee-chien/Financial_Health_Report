# Financial Health Score

## Purpose

The score is the anchor of the whole product.

A client should not need to read all sections to know whether their situation looks healthy.

## Score format

Example:

```text
82 / 100
Excellent
+5 since last review
```

## Suggested dimensions

The overall score can be calculated from weighted sub-scores:

1. Cash Flow
2. Emergency Fund
3. Debt Load
4. Protection
5. Investment Progress
6. Retirement Readiness
7. Estate Planning

## Display

Use a large score on the dashboard.

Below it, show clickable health rings:

- Cash Flow
- Protection
- Investments
- Retirement
- Estate Planning

## Score labels

Suggested labels:

- 90 to 100: Excellent
- 75 to 89: Strong
- 60 to 74: Fair
- 40 to 59: Needs Attention
- Below 40: Urgent Review Needed

## Design requirement

Never show the score alone.

Always show:

- Why the score is what it is
- What improved
- What pulled the score down
- What to review next

## Example explanation

Your cash flow and debt position look strong. The main area to review is protection coverage and long-term planning.

## Expansion behaviour

Default view: simple score and rings.

Expanded view: show score breakdown, calculations, assumptions, and historical trend.
