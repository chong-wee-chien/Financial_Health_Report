# Product Vision

## Vision

Turn a static yearly report into an interactive dashboard that is clear, modern, and useful.

## Product idea

The product should help the client understand their current position, see important changes, and decide what to review next with the adviser.

## What we are avoiding

- Static PDF experience
- Spreadsheet-style screens
- Overloaded tables
- Technical jargon without explanation
- Page-by-page copying of the old report

## What we are building

- A dashboard-first experience
- Simple visual summaries
- Plain-language explanations
- Clickable sections
- Progress views
- Scenario views
- Review-ready action list

## Key questions

The first screen should answer:

1. How am I doing?
2. What changed?
3. What needs attention?
4. What should I review next?

## Long-term direction

The product can grow into a client portal with dashboard, family view, adviser review mode, and guided planning tools.
